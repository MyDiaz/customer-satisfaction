# Airlines Customer Satisfaction Analysis
Identified dissatisfaction drivers and created actionable insights for the product team.

##  Overview

This exploratory analysis is based on a Kaggle dataset about customer satisfaction with Invistico Airlines. The purpose of this report is to present insights for a fictional product team by analyzing the customer experience, identifying service improvement opportunities, and proposing priorities for action.

---
##  Key Findings / Results
- **Finding 1:** 45.27% of customers are dissatisfied with the service.
- **Finding 2:** The top three lowest-rated services among dissatisfied customers are: seat comfort, inflight entertainment and food and drink.
- **Finding 3:** A critical segment identified in the analysis is men under 20 years old who travel for personal reasons in Economy Class.
